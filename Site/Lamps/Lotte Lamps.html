Lotte Lamps are an original Mid-Century Modern product line. They are still produced from the master molds created by Danish immigrants Lotte and Gunnar Bostlund in 1957.

The lamps and lampshades were designed iin the heart of the post-WWII Danish Modern design and crafts movement as the couple applied the principles of modern design to industrial materials.
OUR HISTORY
Lotte Lamps have extra-heavyweight fine stoneware bases, 3-way switches and contemporary colors.

Our fiberglass lampshades have a stylish, sleek cotton finish. They are durable and washable. Also available in linen.

Morten Bostlund carried his parent’s tradition forward and transferred it to Victor and Laura Aume, who are another couple with a passion for ceramics and design.
